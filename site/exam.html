Assessment

Exam

The final assessment asks you to respond to a Request for Proposals by building a visual artifact that demonstrates genuine data exploration. The exam RFP will be shared separately.

What you will produce

  • A case study anchored in a specific territory and issue
  • An angle that connects the case study to a broader argument
  • A visual artifact (map, dashboard, data portal) that demonstrates your data work
  • A story that uses the data to support a convincing proposal
  • A documented process showing how you found, cleaned, and analysed the data

Key principles

  • Show, don't tell. A proposal backed by data analysis is more convincing than one that only describes an approach.
  • AI is a tool, not a replacement for thinking. Use AI to accelerate technical tasks. The reasoning, judgment, and story are yours.
  • Document everything. Your git history, scripts, and methodology are your proof of original work.
  • Tell a clear story. The funder doesn't care about your data volume or technical process. They care that you're making a compelling, grounded case.

The exam RFP and deadline will be communicated separately.
